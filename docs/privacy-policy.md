# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-02

## Overview

نظام النقل is a transport coordination app for fixed local routes in Damietta, Egypt. The app connects passengers with verified drivers, supports trip booking, push notifications, and post-trip ratings.

## Information We Collect

We collect information needed to operate the service:

- Phone number for authentication and account identification.
- Full name and role, such as passenger, driver, or admin.
- Driver information such as national ID, license number, vehicle model, plate, color, and uploaded verification documents.
- Routes, stops, trips, bookings, booking status, seat counts, and ratings.
- Device push notification tokens.
- Crash/error diagnostics if monitoring is enabled.

## How We Use Information

We use this information to:

- Sign users in securely.
- Let drivers publish and manage trips.
- Let passengers search and request bookings.
- Notify users about booking and trip status changes.
- Let passengers contact drivers after accepted bookings.
- Verify driver eligibility.
- Improve reliability and investigate crashes or errors.
- Support admin oversight of pilot operations.

## Sharing

We do not sell personal information.

Limited sharing happens as part of the service:

- A driver can see booking requests for their trips.
- A passenger can see the driver phone number after the booking is accepted.
- Firebase may process authentication and push messaging data.
- Sentry or equivalent monitoring may process crash/error diagnostics if configured.

## Payment

Payments are handled in cash outside the app. The app does not collect payment card, bank, or online payment details.

## Data Retention

We keep operational records while they are needed for service operation, safety, dispute handling, and legal/accounting needs. Device tokens may be deleted when users log out or when Firebase reports them as invalid.

## User Choices

Users can:

- Log out to remove the current device token.
- Contact support to request account or data deletion.
- Disable notifications through Android system settings.

## Security

The backend uses authenticated API sessions. Refresh tokens are stored hashed server-side. Release signing files, Firebase service files, and local environment files must not be committed to source control.
